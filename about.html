Welcome To Our Community!

We are The Community Room — a Studio, Café, and Event Space created to foster creativity, connection, and community.

Rooted in the belief that spaces shape experiences, we designed this environment to feel warm, inspiring, and effortlessly social. It's a place where you can meet, create, host, and unwind all in one.

At the heart of the space is Sweet Mondays, our in-house matcha and coffee concept, serving handcrafted drinks with a focus on quality and aesthetic. What started as a mobile cart has evolved into a permanent part of the studio experience, offering guests a familiar and elevated café feel.

This is more than a café. More than a studio.

It's a space to gather, create, and be part of something.
