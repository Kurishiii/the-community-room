Rooted in Community

The Community Room is a Creative Event Studio + Café designed to bring people together.

We welcome: Small Businesses, Creatives, Community Organizations and anyone looking for a space to gather.

It's a space built for connection — where creativity, community, and everyday experiences meet under one roof. Whether you're stopping in for a matcha, hosting an event, or gathering with others, the space is designed to feel welcoming, flexible, and intentional.

At its core, The Community Room exists to support people.

Sweet Mondays Matcha · Coffee · Community

Cafe Home of SWEET MONDAYS!

Serving matcha, coffee, and specialty drinks designed with intention.

Sweet Mondays brings a soft, elevated café experience into the space — with a focus on quality ingredients, clean flavors, and beautiful presentation.

From daily drinks to event service, Sweet Mondays is the heartbeat of The Community Room. What started as a mobile cart has grown into a full in-house café concept — serving ceremonial grade matcha, handcrafted espresso drinks, cold brews, and seasonal specialties.

Every drink is made with care, and every cup is an experience. Whether you're stopping in for your morning matcha or sipping something special at an event, Sweet Mondays is here for it.
